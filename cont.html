selective accountability will not benefit pakistan

The whole process of accountability will become controversial if Mujahid Kamran’s claims are proven true, said senior anchorperson Haider Waheed. Mujahid Kamran, the former Punjab University vice chancellor who was arrested by NAB last month and was granted bail by the Lahore High Court earlier this month, told SAMAA TV in an interview that the accountability bureau tortures people in custody
“They torture people,” Kamran said. “I saw with my own eyes the torture marks on the body of a man named Haji Nadeem.” He said that Nadeem told him he was hanged by his arm and beaten with sticks.
